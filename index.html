Sonic Fiction is the manual

for your own offworld break-out,

reentry program, for entering Earths

orbit and touching down on the landing strip

of your sense

✧

Meanwhile, creative people --the new peasants--

come to resemble animals converging

on shrinking oases of old media

in a depleted desert.

✧

Because of the compulsion of work and production

we are losing the capacity to play.

We only rarely make playful use of language

we only put it to work.
